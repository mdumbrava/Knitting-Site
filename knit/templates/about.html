About the Owner


Hi! My name is Mihaela and I love to knit. I was born in Romania and I immigrated to Canada in 2003. I started knitting about 3 years ago. I first started with crocheting and I found it was really limiting in the projects you could create. So I learned knitting from a friend and I haven't stopped knitting ever since.

My favourite projects to knit are hats! I really enjoy adding different designs like logos and crests. Really makes the project stand out.

Everthing you see on the site is made by hand so shipping times/available items vary. Please dont hesitate to reach us at our email with any questions.

Thank you for stopping by my site and hopefully you find something here that you enjoy! :)
